Brian Hess Scholarship Fund

Investing in Tomorrow. Building a Stronger Community.

The Brian Hess Scholarship Fund of Putnam County, Inc. recognizes graduating seniors who demonstrate strong character, leadership, integrity, resilience, and a commitment to community.

Apply for Next Cycle Download Overview

Eligibility

Applicants must be graduating seniors enrolled in a Putnam County high school.

Award

One student from each of the five Putnam County high schools is selected to receive a $500 scholarship.

Next Cycle

The 2026 deadline was June 1, 2026. This page is being prepared for next year's application cycle.

Selection Criteria

What the Committee Reviews

Recipients are chosen through a holistic review that may include academic records, a personal essay, recommendations, and evidence of community involvement.

Academic Effort

Consistent dedication to education, demonstrated effort, and a willingness to grow and improve.

Leadership

Participation in school or community activities, with the ability to lead by example and take initiative.

Community Service

A proven commitment to giving back through volunteer work, service, or local involvement.

Character and Integrity

Honesty, responsibility, respect for others, and strong moral character in school and community settings.

Resilience

The ability to overcome challenges, adapt to adversity, and maintain a positive outlook.

Future Goals

A clear sense of direction and motivation to pursue post-secondary education and contribute meaningfully.
