About Sheriff Hess

Leading With Experience

Brian M. Hess brings more than 25 years of law enforcement experience to the Putnam County Sheriff's Office. After serving as Undersheriff and Acting Sheriff, he was elected Putnam County Sheriff in November 2025.

Before joining the Sheriff's Office, Sheriff Hess served with the Westchester County Police Department, where he founded and commanded the Westchester-Putnam Real Time Crime Center. That work connected more than 65 law enforcement agencies and more than 5,500 vetted professionals for real-time intelligence sharing.

New Initiative

The Brian Hess Scholarship Fund of Putnam County, Inc.

The scholarship fund was created to recognize graduating seniors who demonstrate strong character, leadership, integrity, resilience, and a commitment to community.

Each year, one student from each Putnam County high school is selected to receive a $500 scholarship for college, university, trade school, or another accredited post-secondary program.
